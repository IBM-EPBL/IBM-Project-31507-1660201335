A NOVEL METHOD FOR HANDWRITTEN DIGIT RECOGNITION SYSTEM

INSTRUCTION

1.On the home page, you have a next button. You will click the next button to navigate to the recognition page.

2.On the recognition page, you will have an upload option where you can upload the digit image in the format of a scanned image or a document.

3.After uploading the image, click the "recognition" button. Then you will get a predicted result.

4.On this page you have an option that gives the result in voice mode.

5.You have another option to continue the recognition. If you click the continue button, it will redirect to the recognition page.
